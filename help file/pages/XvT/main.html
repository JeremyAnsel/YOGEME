XvT and BoP

Xwing v TIE and its expansion Balance of Power came about and brought the engine forward a few years. A lot of the concourse and engine remained the same, however XvT brought multiplayer capability, a few more ships and better mission design controls.

Sequentially, the change from TIE to XvT is not that bad, however XvT allows for much more depth and precision which XWA later advances further.
